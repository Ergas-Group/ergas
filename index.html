A family company with a peerless reputation for quality, integrity, and excellence.

Ergas Group is the investment group for the Ergas Family. We are comprised of many family owned companies and partnerships, that have been involved in the acquisition, development, and management of commercial and residential properties in the United States and Canada, as well as operating business ventures.

Joseph Ergas emigrated from Santiago, Chile, to build a future for his family in Vancouver, British Columbia. He began acquiring multi-family real estate in key geographic areas across the US, and would later team up with his sons Marcos, Alfonso, and Morris who would help grow and diversify the business. Today, US Multifamily real estate is the core of our business, but we are active in many other asset classes and verticals. As investors of our proprietary capital we have a flexible philosophy to investing and we like to be nimble and opportunistic in our approach.
