ErgasGroup

Ergas Group is the investment group for the Ergas Family. We are comprised of many family-owned companies and partnerships, that are involved in the acquisition, development, management, capital lending and private equity of commercial and residential properties in the United States and Canada. The primary focus of the Ergas Group has been in the acquisition and management of multi-family residential properties across the United States. We seek to acquire well-located multifamily properties in markets with robust fundamentals and high growth potential.

Our diverse investments span other investment styles from venture capital to corporate buyouts across many industries. We have a flexible philosophy to investing, and pursue opportunities that offer capital stewardship and attractive risk reward profiles. As investors of our personal wealth, long-term value creation is our principal goal.
