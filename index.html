About Us

Ergas Group is the investment group for the Ergas Family. We are comprised of many family-owned companies and partnerships, that are involved in the acquisition, development, management, and finance of commercial and residential properties in the United States and Canada. We have a flexible philosophy to investing and, as such, our investment mandate spans other asset classes and verticals that offer capital stewardship and attractive risk reward profiles. As investors of our proprietary capital, long-term value creation is our principal goal.

The Ergas family office continues to carry three generations of representation. The family originates from the Araucaria region of central Chile and has established deep roots in North America after emigrating to Canada’s West Coast. It is imperative to us that our core familial values of integrity, kindness and discipline are always upheld as we contribute to our stakeholders: our partners, customers, and the economy and communities in which we operate.

Areas of Focus

Acquisitions

We actively source, capitalize, and manage real estate investment opportunities in both the United States and Canada. We evaluate investments that offer underlying value creation opportunities, either through value-add strategies or by acquiring well- located assets in robust markets with high growth potential. Our experience spans almost all real estate sectors including office, retail, residential, and industrial, but we have always maintained a heavily concentrated focus in US. Multifamily.

Partnerships

We actively seek real estate development opportunities and partnerships with like- minded organizations who have a track record of consistent investment execution, and a high level of integrity. We offer our partners a diverse expertise in development, acquisitions, and finance. With carefully selected partners, we seek to develop long-standing relationships that lead to successful outcomes

Capital Lending

Ergas Group invests at all levels of the capital structure. With our deep real estate experience, we can understand the needs of our borrowers and are able to provide well structured mortgage loans competitively. We will evaluate First and second mortgages secured by land, a development, or commercial property. In addition, we provide mezzanine loans and other specialty financing that require unique structures

Venture & Private Equity

In addition to real estate, we make investments in early and later stage hyper growth companies. We take and industry agnostic approach, but some of our industries include healthcare, technology, and renewable energy. Depending on the opportunity, we are prepared to make relatively small investments as well as significant equity commitments or a buyout in any one transaction.
